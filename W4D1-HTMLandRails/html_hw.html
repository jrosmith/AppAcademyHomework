Peanut Butter Jelly Time

Description

Classic post kindegarten celebratory or sad collegiate lunchtime meal

Ingredients

  • The butter of at least 50 peanuts
  • The jelly of at least 100 grapes
  • Exactly two slices of wheat bread, crust included
  • Time. If you don't have the time to make a good peanute butter and jelly sandwich, you don't deserve it.

Directions

  1. Remove the head of both your peanut butter and jelly containers
  2. Using a knife, first spread the peanut butter on one slice of bread
  3. On the other slice, spread your jelly
  4. Attach the two pieces of bread with spread sides facing one another
  5. Cut the bread ON A DIAGONAL. This alone affects the flavor the greatest
  6. Take your time and savor every bite, as either a happy child or sad adult.
